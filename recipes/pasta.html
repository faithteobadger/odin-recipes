baked feta pasta recipe

Cherry Tomatoes and a whole block of feta are baked until soft and melty, then tossed with garlic, basil and cooked noodles for a beautifully cheesy dish.

  • 2 pints cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • salt and black pepper
  • rigatoni
  • 1 clove garlic

steps

  1. preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. toss the cherry tomatoes in olive oil and salt
  3. boil your water
  4. as soon as the tomatoes come out of the oven, add in the pasta until evenly coated.
